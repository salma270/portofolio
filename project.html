Feluna Mobile

Feluna is a mobile application designed to support women during their menstrual cycle by combining technology, health, and self-care. It provides a personalized and interactive experience to help users track their cycle, manage period pain, and understand their body better

  • Menstrual Tracker: Predict and monitor your cycle with customizable settings and alerts for irregularities.
  • Stretching Exercises: Guided routines with real-time pose detection AI to ensure correct movement and reduce cramps effectively.
  • Breathing Sessions: Simple breathing exercises to relieve stress and discomfort during menstruation.
  • AI Chatbot Assistant: Ask questions, get personalized advice, and learn more about menstrual health anytime.

Feluna Website

Feluna Website is a responsive web platform that complements the Feluna Mobile App by offering menstrual health support directly from the browser. It includes core features such as a menstrual cycle calendar, breathing exercise guides, and a smart chatbot for personalized assistance—making it easier for users to access Feluna's benefits anytime, without downloading the app.
